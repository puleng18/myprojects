OUTDOOR RESTAURANT

The Outdoor Restaurant offers a unique dining experience amidst the natural beauty of the zoo. Guests can enjoy a variety of dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients while taking in views of the surrounding wildlife.

Some of the highlights of our menu include:

  • Grilled Vegetable Salad - A medley of seasonal vegetables, grilled to perfection and served with a tangy vinaigrette.
  • Wildlife Burger - A juicy beef patty topped with cheddar cheese, lettuce, and tomato, served with a side of fries.
  • Zoo Pizza - Our signature pizza topped with mozzarella, pepperoni, and fresh basil.
  • Fresh Fruit Smoothie - A refreshing blend of seasonal fruits, perfect for a hot day.

Come and relax in our outdoor seating area, where you can watch the animals while enjoying your meal!
