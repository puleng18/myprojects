INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is home to a variety of fascinating insects from around the world. Here, you can learn about the different species of insects, their habitats, and their roles in the ecosystem.

Some of the highlights include:

  • Butterflies - Watch these beautiful creatures flutter around in our butterfly garden.
  • Beetles - Discover the incredible diversity of beetles and their unique adaptations.
  • Ants - Observe the complex social structures of ant colonies.

The Insect House is located near the entrance of the zoo, making it a convenient stop on your visit.
