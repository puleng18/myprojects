PIZZA SHOP

The Pizza Shop offers a variety of delicious pizzas, made with fresh ingredients and baked to perfection. Whether you're in the mood for a classic Margherita or a loaded meat feast, we've got you covered.

Our menu includes:

  • Margherita - Tomato sauce, mozzarella, and fresh basil.
  • Pepperoni - Tomato sauce, mozzarella, and spicy pepperoni.
  • Vegetarian - Tomato sauce, mozzarella, and a medley of fresh vegetables.
  • BBQ Chicken - BBQ sauce, mozzarella, and grilled chicken.
  • Hawaiian - Tomato sauce, mozzarella, ham, and pineapple.

Come and enjoy a slice in our cozy seating area, or grab a pizza to go as you explore the zoo!
