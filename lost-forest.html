LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a mysterious and enchanting area of the zoo, filled with lush vegetation and hidden wonders. As you explore the winding paths, keep an eye out for the diverse wildlife that calls this forest home.

Some of the highlights include:

  • Colorful Birds - Listen to the cheerful songs of the birds as they flit between the trees.
  • Small Mammals - Look for shy creatures like rabbits and foxes hiding in the underbrush.
  • Unique Plants - Discover a variety of plants, some of which are rare and endangered.

The Lost Forest is located in the heart of the zoo, making it a perfect spot to take a break and immerse yourself in nature.
