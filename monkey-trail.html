MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is a fascinating exhibit that allows you to observe various species of monkeys in a naturalistic setting. As you walk along the trail, you'll encounter different types of monkeys swinging through the trees, playing, and interacting with each other.

Some of the highlights include:

  • Spider Monkeys - Watch these agile primates as they navigate the treetops with ease.
  • Capuchin Monkeys - Known for their intelligence, these monkeys often use tools to obtain food.
  • Howler Monkeys - Listen to the distinctive calls of these monkeys, which can be heard for miles.

The Monkey Trail is designed to educate visitors about the importance of primate conservation and the threats these animals face in the wild. Be sure to take your time and enjoy the playful antics of the monkeys!
